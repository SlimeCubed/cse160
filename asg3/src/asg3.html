Controls

Arena Mode

  • WASD: Move
  • Space: Jump
  • Shift: Crouch
  • Escape: Show Menu
  • Right Click: Draw Bow
  • Hold Right Click: Charge Explosive Shot

Creative Mode

  • WASD: Move
  • Space: Fly Up
  • Double Tap Space: Start/Stop Flying
  • Shift: Fly Down
  • Escape: Show Menu
  • Left Click: Break Block
  • Right Click: Place Block
  • 1-9: Switch Block
  • B: Toggle Sphere Brush
  • V: Toggle Fast Placement

Click on the world to get started!
